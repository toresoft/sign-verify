## Summary

The official 523-page reference manual (v6.4, 2024-07-24) for the **Digital Signature Service (DSS)** open-source Java framework, maintained by the European Commission's Digital Building Blocks programme. DSS implements creation, augmentation and â€” most relevant to the sign-verify-2 project â€” **validation** of electronic signatures under the EU **eIDAS** Regulation (910/2014), across all five AdES formats: **XAdES, CAdES, PAdES, JAdES** and **ASiC**. For verification-centric work, the pivotal chapters are **3 (concepts)**, **6 (revocation data)**, **7 (signature validation)**, **10 (augmentation/long-term levels)** and **11 (trusted lists)**. The framework's central validation entry point is `SignedDocumentValidator.fromDocument(doc)`, which auto-selects the format validator from the classpath and returns a `Reports` object exposing `DiagnosticData`, `DetailedReport` (ETSI EN 319 102-1), `SimpleReport`, and the ETSI TS 119 102-2 Validation Report. Validation outcomes follow ETSI indications: **TOTAL-PASSED / TOTAL-FAILED / INDETERMINATE** with defined sub-indications. Certificate qualification is computed at two times (issuance and signing/validation time) per eIDAS Article 32.
